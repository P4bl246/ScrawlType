Welcome to ScrawlType!

Draw whatever you want and convert hand-work into clean and precise digital representations.

Turn hand-written notes into typography text, or convert a hand-drawn diagram into a neat and organized digital format. With ScrawlType, you can easily transform your creative ideas into polished and professional-looking outputs.

About ScrawlType

ScrawlType is a tool designed to bridge the gap between analog creativity and digital precision. We believe handwriting and hand-drawn ideas deserve to live in the digital world without losing their soul.

Our mission

To make digitization of handwritten content accessible, fast, and beautiful for everyone — students, designers, writers, and professionals.
